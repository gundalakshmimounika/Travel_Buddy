Privacy & Policy

TravelBuddy Privacy Guidelines

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect account credentials (your full name, email, and password) directly to configure and personalize your TravelBuddy experiences. GPS location data is accessed securely in real-time only to provide custom local weather, upcoming trip statuses, and nearby map coordinates.

2. AI Assistant & Chat Data

Any conversations or inquiries with our Chat AI companion are handled dynamically on encrypted database records. We analyze user input strictly to curate smart, high-quality answers and recommendations, ensuring your conversations are never shared with advertising entities.

3. Database & Local Protection

All user accounts, password updates, session states are securely registered in our SQL tables on our backend. We utilize advanced validation patterns on emails and passwords to ensure account integrity, keeping your personal credentials completely isolated and private.

4. Razorpay Secure Gateways

All payment bookings, tickets, and financial transactions are routed directly via official SDK integrations (e.g. Razorpay). TravelBuddy does not store your credit card or CVV details, maintaining a 100% compliance record with secure payment guidelines.

5. Contact & Compliance

TravelBuddy reserves the right to modify these guidelines to align with Google Play and secure framework policies. For any inquiries regarding location usage or data deletion options, please navigate to our Help & Support section.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I plan a new trip?

Go to the Trips section in the bottom search layout or explore hot tourist spots inside the TravelBuddy community forum to add custom destinations.

Is the AI Chat Buddy free?

Yes! Your smart Chat Companion is 100% free and ready 24/7 to draft personalized schedules, forecast packing requirements, and look up weather indices.

How do I configure my packing list?

Tap your active destination card, open the Packing checklist, add custom items, check items as you pack, or ask TravelBuddy AI to draft a smart default list based on your destination!
